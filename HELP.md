## Overview
ReceiptVault is a Next.js application for uploading and viewing digital receipts and operational invoices.

## Features
- **Fast Uploads**: Drag-and-drop interface for quick receipt intake.
- **Secure Viewing**: Optimized view modes for reading small-print receipt data.
- **Modern Stack**: Built with Next.js 14 and TypeScript.

## How to Use
1. Upload your digital receipt or invoice via the upload screen.
2. Navigate to the vault to view and categorize your saved receipts.
3. Search for specific transactions by date or merchant name.
