## Overview
Wayfinder is a progress and bookmark tracking application for navigating complex operational waypoints.

## Features
- **Waypoint Tracking**: Monitor your progress through multi-step operational flows.
- **Netlify Functions**: Serverless backend for managing bookmarks and waypoints.
- **Progress Persistence**: Automatically saves your position in the waypoint sequence.

## How to Use
1. Start a new Wayfinder session to begin tracking your operational progress.
2. Bookmark essential waypoints for quick return during complex tasks.
3. Review your progress log to ensure all milestones have been reached.
