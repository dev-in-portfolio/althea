## Overview
ReceiptBox is a specialized organizer for digital receipts, invoices, and operational expenses.

## Features
- **Digital Archive**: Securely store and categorize receipt data.
- **Search & Filter**: Find specific expenses by date or category.
- **Renderer-Based**: Fast, interactive UI for desktop-style receipt management.

## How to Use
1. Upload or input receipt data into the workspace.
2. Assign categories and tags to each entry.
3. Review your expense history in the chronological log.
