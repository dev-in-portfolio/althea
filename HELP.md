## Overview
This application translates restaurant BOH/FOH terminology into tech and engineering equivalents (and vice versa).

## Features
- **Bi-Directional Translation**: Swap between Restaurant and Tech contexts.
- **Netlify Functions**: Backend support for custom terms and favorites.
- **Local-First**: Works offline with localStorage persistence.

## How to Use
1. Select your translation direction (Restaurant -> Tech or Tech -> Restaurant).
2. Search or browse for specific terms.
3. Save frequently used translations to your favorites list.
