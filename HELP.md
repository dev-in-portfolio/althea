## Overview
PalmLedger is a high-capacity ledger system capable of handling up to 200,000 entries and 500 categories.

## Features
- **Massive Scalability**: Designed for high-volume operational logging.
- **Categorization**: Organize entries into hundreds of distinct categories.
- **Express/Postgres**: Backed by a high-performance Node.js and PostgreSQL stack.

## How to Use
1. Select a category for your new entry.
2. Input the title, note, and unit value.
3. Use the filters to navigate through large datasets of historical logs.
