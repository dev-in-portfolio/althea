## Overview
CueDeck is a robust system for creating, managing, and organizing massive collections of digital cards and decks.

## Features
- **High Capacity**: Supports up to 500 decks and 50,000 cards per deck.
- **Tagging System**: Organize cards with custom tags (up to 20 per card).
- **Postgres Backend**: Reliable data persistence for large datasets.

## How to Use
1. Create a new deck from the main dashboard.
2. Add cards with titles, descriptions, and tags.
3. Search and filter your decks via the central management interface.
