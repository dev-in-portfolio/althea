## Overview
In measure theory, a branch of mathematics, the Lebesgue measure, named after French mathematician Henri Lebesgue, is the standard way of assigning a measure to subsets of higher dimensional Euclidean n-spaces. For lower dimensions n = 1 , 2 , or 3 {\displaystyle n=1,2,{\text{or }}3} , it coincides with the standard measure of length, area, or volume.
