## Restaurant
In a professional kitchen, 'SOP' represents a specific operational concept tied to management & metrics. In technology environments, this maps to 'Runbook', highlighting similar principles of system reliability, workflow coordination, and performance optimization. Understanding this concept helps teams design processes that are resilient, efficient, and scalable.
