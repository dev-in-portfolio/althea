## Restaurant
In a professional kitchen, 'ServSafe mindset' represents a specific operational concept tied to safety & compliance. It reflects how teams coordinate work, manage resources, and maintain flow under real-time pressure. In technology environments, this maps to 'Compliance culture', highlighting similar principles of system reliability, workflow coordination, and performance optimization. Understanding this concept helps teams design processes that are resilient, efficient, and scalable.
